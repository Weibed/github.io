Ladder Truck

--Truck Features--

Excellent Horizontal and Vertical Reach
- The ladder is built with 100,000 psi yield strength steel allowing firefighters to safely reach taller structures.
12' and 14' Narrow Outrigger Spread
- The outriggers are designed with a automatic locking system so there is no need for pins to be placed or removed.
Maximum Pump Capabilites
- Up to 2,250 GPM pump to deliver water 110' above the ground.
Standard Equipment
- The name of the truck says it all, it has ladders. There are hydraulic ladders and manual ladders. A ladder truck will also have much of the same smaller tools that and Fire Engine would equip.

What Does A Fire Department Use A Ladder Truck For?

The most common use for a ladder truck can seem pretty self explainable. Reach spaces where a fire might be to high for a standard ladder and a fire fighter to reach. While this is true it is not the only reason a ladder truck is useful. Another use can be for saftey. Using a ladder truck you can position yourself upwind and out of the way of the smoke and flames. You are also separated from the building incase the structure begins to get weak and collapse. They are not only useful in big cities with tall sky scrappers but alos in rural residential areas where they might be an attic fire that can be fought from above or use it to stop the spread of the fire to surrounding buildings or vegitation.
