What Does A Fire Department Use A Tanker For?

The tanker is a very import piece of equipment for any department in rural areas. The main reason it is so important is because it can transport large amounts of water to a fire scene where there are no fire hydrants to connect to. The tanker will drive back and forth from the fire scene to unload its water supply into a drop tank and then drive to the nearest fire hydrant or body of water to refill so that the fire fighters on the scene do not run out of water.
