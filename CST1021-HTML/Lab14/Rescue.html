What Does A Fire Department Use A Rescue Truck For?

Usually fire departments will have two types of rescue trucks. One style will either be a modified pickup truck or SUV with plenty of compartments for storing medical equipment and the second style is what we see here in this picture. The pickup truck or SUV will most likely be the first vehicle to leave in a personal injury incident usually allowing 5 first responders to occupy the vehicle and get to the scene very quickly. On bigger incidents where many responders are necessary the larger rescue will be used to transport more people. There is also a lot more resources and equipment that can be brought to the scene with the larger rescue truck.
